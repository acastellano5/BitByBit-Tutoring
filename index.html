Mission

Welcome to BitByBit Tutoring! My mission is to make coding education accessible to everyone, one step at a time. With personalized 1 on 1 coding sessions offered via Zoom every day from 5 to 10 pm, I'm here to guide you through your coding journey, bit by bit. What sets BitByBit Tutoring apart is its commitment to inclusivity - sessions are completely free, ensuring that learning the ropes of programming is within everyone's reach. Whether you're looking to delve into the world of Python or explore the intricacies of web development, I'm ready to assist. To embark on your coding adventure, simply reach out to me at bitbybittutoring@gmail.com and schedule your session. Let's code, learn, and grow together!
